Malnutrition: A Disease That no one cares about

  Malnutrition refers to deficiencies or excesses in nutrient intake, imbalance of essential nutrients or impaired nutrient utilization.

Undernutrition

Stunting

  Stunting is the impaired growth and development that children experience from poor nutrition, repeated infection, and inadequate psychosocial stimulation.

Wasting

  Wasting is the most immediate, visible and life-threatening form of malnutrition. It results from the failure to prevent malnutrition among the most vulnerable children

UnderWeight

  Underweight is defined as low weight-for-age. Low BMI is caused by an unbalanced diet or an underlying disease that affects nutrient absorption.

OverWeight

  Overweight is a form of malnutrition. Malnutrition is caused by eating a diet in which nutrients are insufficient or too much such that it causes health problems.
